The Historic Journey

The Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Mandir marks the culmination of a centuries-long wait for millions of devotees. Following the landmark Supreme Court verdict in 2019, the foundation stone (Bhoomi Pujan) was laid by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on August 5, 2020. The grand Pran Pratishtha (consecration) of the Ram Lalla idol took place on January 22, 2024, ushering in a new era for Ayodhya.

Architectural Marvel

Designed by the Sompura Family, the temple is a stunning example of the Nagara Style of temple architecture.

  • Dimensions: 380 feet long, 250 feet wide, and 161 feet high.
  • Structure: It features 3 stories, 392 pillars, and 44 doors.
  • No Iron: The temple is built entirely using pink sandstone from Rajasthan, without the use of iron or steel, ensuring a lifespan of over 1,000 years.
