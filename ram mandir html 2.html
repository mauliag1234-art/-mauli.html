INFORMATION ABOUT RAM MANDIR

Ram Mandir is a grand Hindu temple located in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh. It is believed to be built at the birthplace of Lord Shri Ram, one of the most revered deities in Hinduism. The temple represents faith, devotion, and India’s rich cultural heritage.

What is Ram Mandir:

Ram Mandir is a sacred temple dedicated to Lord Ram. It is constructed in traditional Nagara style architecture and stands as a symbol of spiritual and cultural unity.

Why It is Important:

The temple holds immense religious significance for millions of devotees. It reflects centuries-old beliefs, historical events, and the cultural identity of India.

Key Features:

• Traditional Indian temple architecture
• Intricate stone carvings and sculptures
• Dedicated sanctum for Lord Ram
• Large complex for devotees and pilgrims

Location:

Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, India
