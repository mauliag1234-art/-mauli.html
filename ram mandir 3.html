About Shri Ram Mandir

Shri Ram Mandir in Ayodhya is a sacred Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shri Ram, the seventh avatar of Lord Vishnu. It symbolizes faith, devotion, and cultural heritage.

History

Ayodhya is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Shri Ram. The Ram Mandir stands as a historic and spiritual landmark representing centuries of devotion and tradition.

Darshan & Worship

Devotees from across the world visit the temple for darshan, aarti, and spiritual peace. The temple architecture reflects ancient Indian craftsmanship.
